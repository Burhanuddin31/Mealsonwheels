    This is a must visit place for those who visit Bessy! We’ve tried mostly everything from their menu and everything was 10/10 I would say. The chicken pasta and the mft chicken burger is a must try. Today was my 5th visit to the madras food truck and as usual the taste and the quality of the food was maintained well. And most importantly its affordable as well. If you pass by Bessy do check it out! I’m sure you’ll love it
  • 4/5

    If you ever go to besant Nagar Beach then definitely visit this food truck that's located in the walkable distance from the beach. You can enjoy a limited but very tasty range of veg items. We tried all the 4 items from the menu all were extremely good. However the portion of food is little for the price we pay.
  • 4/5

    Visited this food truck on my last visit to Beasant Nagar. It's just near to Elliot's Beach. They had good variety of sea food and also some unique snacks like Chilli lotus Stem. Taste was 5/5 and even the quantity they served was more than enough. The prices were well justified and reasonable. I really loved their big show burger and strongly recommend it. Also the truck itself was cute and attractive!
